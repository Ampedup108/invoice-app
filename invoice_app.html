Field Invoice Manager

Connect your Google Sheet in 3 steps.

  1. Go to Google Sheets, create a new blank sheet named Field Invoice Manager
  2. Click Extensions → Apps Script, delete existing code, paste the AppsScript.gs file, click Save
  3. Click Deploy → New Deployment → Web App, set Execute as: Me, Who has access: Anyone, click Deploy, copy the URL

Your data stays in your own Google Sheet.
